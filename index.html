About me

I am an avid coder and a free software enthusiast. I started hacking in early 2013 on Natural Language Processing utilities as a member of the language computing community Swathanthra Malayalam Computing(SMC). My current major open source contribution is for the PeARS Project - which is an ongoing effort to build a fully distributed, p2p search engine.


Originally from Kerala, I currently like in Milan, Italy. I build scalable, highly available infrastructure for fun and profit. I lead the tech team at a growing startup called Quick Algorithm. Other tech interests of mine include distributed systems, programming languages and compilers. I feel home coding in Bash, Python, and Clojure.
